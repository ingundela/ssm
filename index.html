About Us

SSM Auditores & Consultores, Lda. is an emerging company in the Mozambican market with dynamic and innovative attitude to better serve the clients.

SSM Auditores e Consultores, Lda. has been operating in the national market for 20 years, of which the first 10 years as an individual partner services, 7 years as a small Accounting and Audit company, and 3 years as an Medium and Merging Company in the Mozambican market.

Having been officially registered as SSM Auditores & Consultores, Lda. in 2015, where the company achieved the economic and financial stability capable of justifying the legal necessity and existence for some of its clients.

SSM, Auditores e Consultores, Lda. has about 20 employees and is currently encouraging the development and integration of women in their professional careers.
